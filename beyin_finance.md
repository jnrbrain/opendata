# Beyin Finance Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the collection, processing, storage, and security of user data by Beyin Finance and is in compliance with applicable legal regulations, including the Capital Markets Board (CMB) regulations, GDPR, and other relevant laws. By utilizing the services provided by Beyin Finance, users acknowledge and accept the terms set forth in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Scope and Objective

This Privacy Policy sets forth the measures taken by Beyin Finance to ensure the privacy and security of personal, financial, and application data collected from users. The data handling procedures comply with applicable regulations, including the Capital Markets Law (CML) and other relevant laws. Data is collected, processed, and stored only to the extent necessary for the provision of services, legal compliance, and improving platform performance.

---

## 2. Data Collected

Beyin Finance may collect the following types of data:

- **Personal Information:** Full name, email address, user identifiers, and other identification details for account creation and regulatory compliance.  
- **Financial Information:** Transaction history, trade volumes, profits, losses, and other financial data necessary to execute trades and generate reports.  
- **API Keys:** Collected solely to execute trades on behalf of users via their Binance accounts.  
- **Application Information and Performance:** Crash logs, diagnostics, and other app performance metrics.  
- **Application Activity:** In-app actions, search history, installed apps, and other activity logs.  
- **Device or Other Identifiers:** Device IDs, operating system, and other device-related identifiers.  
- **Location Information:** Approximate location when required for compliance or service purposes.

---

## 3. Purpose and Legal Basis for Data Processing

Beyin Finance processes user data under the following lawful bases:

- **Legal Compliance:** Compliance with regulatory obligations, including reporting to the Capital Markets Board (CMB) and risk management.  
- **Contractual Necessity:** Execution of trades, account management, and service provision.  
- **Legitimate Interests:** Improving services, performing market analysis, developing new features, and securing the platform.  
- **User Consent:** For marketing, notifications, or other optional activities outside of legal, contractual, or legitimate interest purposes.

---

## 4. Sharing of Data with Third Parties

Beyin Finance will not disclose user data to third parties except in the following cases:

- **Legal Requirements:** Requests from regulators, authorities, or legal obligations, including the Capital Markets Board (CMB).  
- **Service Providers:** Third-party providers who perform services on behalf of Beyin Finance (cloud hosting, technical support, financial services).  
- **Business Transfers:** Mergers, acquisitions, or sale of assets where data transfer is necessary and protected under this Privacy Policy.

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

- Data is retained **only as long as necessary** for legal compliance or service provision.  
- Users can request deletion of their account and associated data via [https://beyinfinance.com/contact-us/](https://beyinfinance.com/contact-us/).  
- Some data may be automatically deleted over time according to the app’s internal retention policies.

---

## 6. User Rights

Under GDPR and applicable laws, users have the following rights:

- **Right to Access:** Users can request access to their personal data.  
- **Right to Rectification:** Users can correct or update inaccurate or incomplete data.  
- **Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”):** Users can request deletion of data.  
- **Right to Restriction of Processing:** Users can limit processing under certain conditions.  
- **Right to Object:** Users can object to processing for legitimate interests or marketing.  
- **Right to Data Portability:** Users can request structured, machine-readable transfer of data.

---

## 7. Data Security Measures

- **Encryption:** Sensitive data, including API keys, is encrypted during storage and transmission.  
- **Secure Transmission:** Data is transmitted over secure connections.  
- **Access Control:** Access is restricted to authorized personnel with internal audits.  
- **Regular Security Audits:** Security systems and procedures are periodically reviewed.

---

## 8. User Notification and Transparency

Beyin Finance ensures transparency by providing users with clear and comprehensive information regarding data collection and processing practices. Users will be notified of any changes to this Privacy Policy, which will be updated periodically. Notifications will be sent via website, app, or other communication channels.

---

## 9. Data Breach Notifications

In the event of a data breach that compromises the confidentiality or integrity of user data, Beyin Finance will notify affected users in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR. Since users register anonymously, notifications will be provided via the app or website.
